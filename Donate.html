Support The Garden School.

The Garden School operates entirely through community support. Donations keep our classes free, fund workshop materials, provide meals, and help us expand our reach to more young people who need a soft place to land.

Why your support matters

Your gift covers books, test fees, groceries for weekly dinners, art and sewing supplies, carpentry materials, and stipends for community educators and mentors.

We are a registered 501(c)(3), and all contributions are tax-deductible.

Donate Now

Don’t have funds but want to help? Reach out about volunteering, hosting a workshop, or sponsoring supplies.

A few examples of what your support can make possible:

  • A month of groceries for community dinners.
  • GED test fees for a student ready to sit their exams.
  • Art, sewing, or woodworking materials for a full workshop cycle.
  • Stipends for guest educators with lived experience and local knowledge.
