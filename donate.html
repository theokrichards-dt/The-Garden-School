Where Your Donation Goes

Every dollar supports real costs: food, materials, space, and the people who show up for students.

We don’t charge tuition. We don’t require fees. We believe education should be accessible, dignified, and free. That’s only possible because of people like you.

GED Materials Textbooks, practice tests, online resources, and test registration fees.
Weekly Meals Fresh ingredients for community dinners and cooking workshops.
Workshop Supplies Tools, fabric, wood, art materials, and equipment for hands-on learning.
Instructor Stipends Fair compensation for teachers, mentors, and workshop leaders.
Space & Utilities Keeping our home-based learning space safe, comfortable, and accessible.
Program Expansion Growing our capacity to serve more students and deepen our offerings.
