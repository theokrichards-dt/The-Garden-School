Rooted in care, growing toward opportunity

The Garden School is an educational program of The Garden Collective, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit in Hamden, Connecticut. We walk alongside youth navigating instability, court involvement, and reentry—pairing GED instruction with the mentorship and belonging every young person deserves.

Holistic approach

Academic support meets social-emotional learning, creative expression, and practical life skills. We respond to the whole person.

Small by design

Intentionally intimate cohorts mean every student is seen, known, and supported with personalized plans.

Community partnerships

Local mentors, artists, counselors, and tradespeople walk with our students, opening doors to possibilities.
